Hello, I am QuickyFy Weather.

Your all time Weather Companion!

QuickyFy Weather is a service to provide us with Current Weather for our desired location within seconds. It can give us weather details for our given location or can detect location automatically using our location access for latitude and longitude. This service is entirely based on Basic JS and designed with Love in Bootstrap 4.
